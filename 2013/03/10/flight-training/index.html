VFR, marginal VFR, special VFR (clearance), IFR

  1. VFR 3000+ feet cloud bottom, 5 miles visibility
  2. Marginal VFR 1000 ~ 3000 feet cloud bottom, 3~5 statute miles visibilty
  3. Special VFR 1000 feet, 3 statute miles visibility
  4. IFR 1000- feet, 1 statute mile

Generally speaking, for VFR and MVFR, I can decide whether I can fly or not.
For IFR, it is definitely a No-No for flight. For Special VFR, it can be required from
tower controller at B, C, D, E airport.

forward slip:

  • power to idle, leave nose down
  • maintain 85 knots, no flap
  • right bank and left rudder, or opposite based on situation
  • foot step on the topwind

emergency decent:

  • throttle to idle
  • speed 99 knots
  • full flap

Engine Failure

  • maintain best glide speed, 65 knots, with trim set
  • looking for a place to land
  • check fuel selector to Both, fuel cut-off valve is IN, mixture is RICH
    try to restart the engine, IF failed, fuel selector to LEFT, fuel cut-off value is OUT, mixture Cut-Off
  • transponder 7700, frequency 121.5 or current frequency, declare Mayday Emergency, Call Sign, Location, Altitude

engine runup:

  • rpm 1800 to idle
  • correct gyro compass

pilot currency requirements:

take passenger

  • three takeoff and landing (T/O and LNDG)
  • if at the night, it must be full stop

steep turn

  • look outside, refer to horizon for 45 degrees.
  • smoothly apply rudder when bank to 45 and turn.

pattern work

  • controlled airport
    inbound with Information XXX
    XXX tower/approach, call sign XXX at [distance] [direction]

Takeoff: 700 feet+ turn to crosswind
at mid downwind, report at mid field
pass thredhold, reduce power, pull the yoke, slow down to 85knots
flap to 10, nose down, add some power to 1500rpm

to Base, 75 knot, 20 flap
to final, 65 knot, 30 flap, keep 65 knot

maintain aiming at the aim point, keep 65 knot, stable approach, add power when necessary, fly to the runway.

  • uncontrolled airport

    10 sm , initial radio contact
    3 sm, report
    each leg report
    clear of runway report

soft field take-off /LNDG

  • yoke all the way back
  • 10`C flap
  • 57` climbing speed
  • like normal landing

short field take-off / LNDG

  • 10`C flap
  • with feet on brake, full power on starting line
  • release the feet on brake
  • rotate at 51`C, climb at 57`C
  • lower the nose, let the speed build up, flap up, max rate climb speed
  • landing, flap up, apply full breake gently

slow flight

  • maintain heading
  • hold altitude

vacuum system and instrument

  • attitude indicator
  • directional indicator
  • power (pwr) to 1800 rpm, when horn comes on

pitot-static system and instrument

  • function
  • compoents
  • instruments
    ++ airspeed indicator
    ++ vertical speed indicator
    ++ altimeter

stall

power-off stall

  • power off stall, flap down should not raise the altitude
  • climb at stall recovery
  • flap 30, 20, 10

power on stall

  • no flap
  • power to 1500
  • speed down to take off speed
  • full power
  • use right rudder

  1. In take
  2. Compression

two exceptions

Detonation: is uncontrolled explosive ignition, caused by fue/air mixture
execessive temperature, overhead/lower fuel grade

Preignition: is in advance of the normal timed ignition. caused by hot spot in
cylinder, carbon deposit, carcked ceramic spark plug
